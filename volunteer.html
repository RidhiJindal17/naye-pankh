Why Volunteer?

Joining NayePankh Foundation is more than simple community service. It’s an avenue to discover your potential and build professional competence.

Leadership Skills

Lead field operations, direct campaign logistics, coordinate regional donation drives, and manage student chapters independently.

Networking

Collaborate with over 10,000+ passionate student campaigners, chapter leads, and advisory members across top Indian universities.

Social Impact

Participate directly in slum coaching libraries, hygiene pad distribution, and animal feeding drives to create real-world relief.

Community Building

Help bridge structural social inequalities in urban settings, and foster empathy and mutual respect in underprivileged blocks.

Volunteer Process

Getting started is quick and easy. Our structured onboarding ensures you start impacting lives efficiently.

Step 1

Register

Submit your application details using the registration form below, selecting your preferred chapter city and social program.

Step 2

Orientation

Attend our interactive online welcoming seminar to understand NayePankh's operating values, reporting patterns, and field safety protocols.

Step 3

Join Projects

Get assigned to active local field campaigns (e.g. food distribution or street teaching) under a City Chapter Coordinator.

Step 4

Create Impact

Carry out field operations, compile monthly updates, and receive official leadership certificates and performance LORs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about volunteering? Find answers below.

Who can register as a volunteer for NayePankh?

+

Our volunteer chapters consist primarily of high-school, college, and university students. However, we welcome anyone, including corporate professionals, who is eager to dedicate weekends to community drive campaigns.

Do volunteers receive a certificate of completion?

+

Yes. Upon completing a minimum commitment duration (usually 1 month) and logging active field updates, NayePankh Foundation issues official, verified Internship Certificates. High performers also receive custom Letters of Recommendation (LOR).

Is there a fee to register or participate?

+

Absolutely not. NayePankh Foundation is a registered NGO. Sponsoring certificates, logistics equipment, and orientations are 100% free of charge for students. We strictly prohibit onboarding fees.

Can I volunteer part-time or remotely?

+

Yes. Our field campaigns are organized on weekend mornings to avoid conflict with school or college schedules. Additionally, we run virtual internship modules in Graphic Design, Social Media Management, Content Writing, and Fundraising.
