Frequently Asked Questions

Have some questions about our operations, certificates, or donation channels? Read our quick FAQs below.

Is my donation to NayePankh tax-exempt?

+

Yes. NayePankh Foundation is registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 80G and 12A. Any donation made by an Indian taxpayer is eligible for a 50% tax deduction. We generate tax-exempt receipts automatically.

Where are my donation funds utilized?

+

Over 95% of our receipts go directly to purchase food ingredients, sanitary pads, educational books, notebooks, bags, and strays' veterinary support. Because we are completely run by student volunteers, our administrative overheads are kept under 5%.

Who makes up the NayePankh Foundation?

+

NayePankh is one of the largest student-led NGOs in India. Our core committees, city coordinators, field volunteers, and developers consist entirely of passionate high-school and college students who wish to drive social change.

Can I intern remotely/from home?

+

Yes. We offer virtual internships in graphic design, social media marketing, content writing, HR coordination, and virtual fundraising. You can apply on our Donate & Join portal page.
